Browser-Based Painting!

The purpose of this page is to demonstrate the ability to make dynamic painting possible in browsers using just JS and some tricky code. Feel free to experiment, draw, save, and do whatever you like with this proof-of-concept.

Note: The paint bucket tool was planned for implementation, but when I tried to get the math to work by replacing every pixel that failed a specific check, I rapidly ran into a browser crashing bug. Turns out, doing tons and tons of math to check every adjacent pixel and replace it with the selected color is not the proper way to implement this feature! I figured it wasn't super important and left it behind as an artifact of failed effort.

The fill feature is not working yet.
